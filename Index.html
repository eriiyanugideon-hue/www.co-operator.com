About Us

Welcome to Co Operator, a story company founded by a 10-year-old visionary, Sanyaolu Eriiyanuoluwa Gideon Olanrewaju (EGO). Established in 2026, our company specializes in creating engaging stories and narratives that inspire imagination and creativity.

As a young entrepreneur, EGO brings fresh perspectives and innovative ideas to storytelling. Our mission is to craft compelling tales that resonate with audiences of all ages, fostering a love for reading and storytelling in the digital age.

We value inclusivity and safety, which is why we have implemented age-appropriate access controls to ensure our platform is suitable for users aged 10 and above, aligning with our founder's age and vision.

We have a simple idea: to create a space where stories could come to life in the most imaginative ways. Our passion for storytelling and creativity led to the birth of Co Operator, a company that celebrates the power of narratives and the joy of sharing stories with the world.

This all began with a 5 year old EGO who loved to write stories. No-one knows who he got it from maybe his Paternal grandfather or his father, but his father was happy to have a future author in his house.

The Birth of a Dream

At just 5 years old, EGO discovered the magic of storytelling. While other children played with toys, young Gideon spent hours crafting tales of adventure, mystery, and wonder. His imagination knew no bounds, and his stories began to fill notebooks that his father proudly preserved.

As EGO grew, so did his passion. By age 8, he had written dozens of stories, each one more elaborate than the last. He dreamed of a world where everyone could share their stories freely, where creativity wasn't limited by age or experience. This vision became the foundation of what would become Co Operator.

In 2026, at the tender age of 10, EGO took the bold step of founding his own company. With determination beyond his years, he assembled a team of like-minded individuals who shared his belief in the power of storytelling. Together, they built a platform that would revolutionize how stories are created, shared, and experienced.

Our Mission and Vision

Co Operator exists to democratize storytelling. We believe that every person, regardless of age, background, or experience, has a story worth telling. Our mission is to provide the tools, platform, and community that empower storytellers worldwide.

Our vision is a world where imagination thrives, where stories connect people across cultures and generations, and where creativity is celebrated as the most valuable human endeavor. We strive to create a safe, inclusive space where voices that might otherwise go unheard can find their audience.

The Co Operator Difference

What sets Co Operator apart is our unique perspective. Founded by a child for storytellers of all ages, we understand the pure joy of creation. We reject the notion that great stories can only come from established authors or professionals. Instead, we celebrate the raw creativity that exists in every heart.

Our platform features intuitive tools designed with both beginners and experienced writers in mind. From simple story starters to advanced narrative structures, we provide everything needed to bring ideas to life. And because we believe in accessibility, all our premium features are completely free.

Community First

At Co Operator, community is everything. We've built more than just a writing platform; we've created a family of storytellers. Our users range from children discovering their voice for the first time to seasoned authors exploring new genres.

We host regular writing challenges, story circles, and creative workshops. Our community guidelines emphasize respect, encouragement, and constructive feedback. Every story published on our platform receives the attention and appreciation it deserves.

Innovation Through Youth

Being founded by a 10-year-old brings unique advantages to Co Operator. We see the world through fresh eyes, unburdened by industry conventions or outdated practices. This youthful perspective drives our innovation:

  • Intuitive Design: Interfaces that feel natural, even to those new to technology
  • Gamified Learning: Writing challenges and achievements that make skill-building fun
  • Inclusive Features: Tools designed with accessibility and diverse needs in mind
  • Future-Focused: Always anticipating how technology can enhance storytelling

Our Growth Story

From a single child's dream to a thriving creative community, Co Operator's journey has been nothing short of remarkable. In our first year, we welcomed thousands of writers from around the world. Our story archive grew exponentially as users discovered the joy of sharing their narratives.

We've expanded our platform to include comic creation tools, allowing stories to come alive in visual form. Our writing workshops have helped countless individuals overcome writer's block and develop their skills. And through it all, our commitment to free, accessible creativity has never wavered.

Looking to the Future

As Co Operator continues to grow, our vision expands. We plan to introduce collaborative storytelling features, international language support, and partnerships with schools and libraries worldwide. Our goal is to make storytelling a universal language that connects humanity.

The future of Co Operator is bright, driven by the same childlike wonder that started it all. We invite you to join us on this journey of imagination and creativity.
